Welcome to the TokenFabric SDK documentation. Our SDK is designed to empower developers and businesses to seamlessly integrate the TokenFabric protocol into their own applications and services.

<Note>
  The TokenFabric SDK is currently under active development. This documentation will be updated regularly as new features are implemented and finalized.
</Note>

## Overview

The TokenFabric SDK allows you to leverage our powerful token creation and management capabilities within your own projects. By integrating our SDK, you can:

- Create and manage digital assets (ERC20 and ERC1155 tokens) programmatically
- Implement custom bonding curves and tokenomics models
- Access TokenFabric's liquidity pools and trading functions
- Utilize our cross-chain bridging capabilities

## Benefits of Integration

By integrating the TokenFabric SDK into your project, you'll benefit from:

1. **Rapid Development**: Accelerate your blockchain project development with our battle-tested infrastructure.
2. **Flexibility**: Customize token behavior and economics to fit your specific use case.
3. **Scalability**: Leverage TokenFabric's robust architecture to support high-volume operations.
4. **Revenue Sharing**: Participate in our protocol fee redistribution program for partners.
