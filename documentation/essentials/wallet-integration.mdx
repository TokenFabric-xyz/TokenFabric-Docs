# Wallet Integration with TokenFabric

TokenFabric offers flexible wallet integration options, allowing users to interact with our platform using their preferred method. Whether you're a seasoned crypto user with an existing wallet or new to the blockchain space, we've got you covered.

## Connection Options

### 1. Use Your Existing Wallet

TokenFabric supports a wide range of existing wallets, making it easy for users to connect and start creating or trading digital assets immediately.

- **Supported Wallets**: Over 350 wallets compatible
- **Popular Options**: 
  - MetaMask
  - Coinbase Wallet
  - WalletConnect
  - And many more!

### 2. Create a New Wallet Instantly

For users new to blockchain or those preferring a streamlined experience, TokenFabric offers the ability to create a new wallet within seconds.

- **Email Sign-up**: Create a wallet using just your email address
- **Google Authentication**: Use your Google account for quick wallet creation
- **No Downloads Required**: Our in-app wallet works directly in your browser

## Connecting Your Wallet

1. Navigate to the TokenFabric dApp
2. Click on the "Connect Wallet" button
3. Choose from the following options:
   - Select your existing wallet from the list of supported providers
   - Click "Social Login" and follow the prompts for email or Google sign-up

## Security and Control

- **Non-Custodial**: Whether you use an existing wallet or create a new one through our dApp, you always maintain full control of your private keys
- **Industry-Standard Security**: Our wallet creation process adheres to the highest security standards in the blockchain industry

## Seamless Integration

Once connected, you can seamlessly:
- Create new digital assets (ERC20 tokens or ERC1155 NFTs)
- Trade on our bonding curve markets
- Manage your assets
- Participate in the TokenFabric ecosystem

## Advanced Usage

For developers and projects looking to integrate TokenFabric directly:

- **Direct Contract Interaction**: Advanced users can interact directly with our smart contracts on the blockchain
- **Partner Integrations**: We offer solutions for partners to integrate TokenFabric functionality into their own platforms
