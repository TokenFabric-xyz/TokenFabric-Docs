# TokenFabric Affiliation Program

Welcome to the TokenFabric Affiliation Program, where community growth meets rewarding opportunities. Our program is designed to incentivize users who help expand our ecosystem by referring new members to the platform.

## Program Overview

The TokenFabric Affiliation Program allows you to earn a portion of the transaction fees generated by users you refer to our platform. By sharing your unique referral link, you can start building your network and earning rewards.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Referral Link">
    Once you've connected your wallet to TokenFabric, you'll receive a unique referral link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share Your Link">
    Spread the word about TokenFabric and share your referral link with potential new users.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Users Join Through Your Link">
    When a new user creates a wallet or connects for the first time using your referral link, they're registered as your referral.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Earn Rewards">
    You'll start earning a portion of the transaction fees typically captured by the protocol from your referrals' activities.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Reward Structure

Our reward structure is designed to benefit all participants while providing extra incentives for top performers.

### Standard Rewards
All affiliates earn a percentage of the transaction fees generated by their referrals. This creates a continuous stream of rewards as long as your referrals remain active on the platform.

## Reward Distribution

Currently, rewards are distributed periodically through fixed-time drops. We are working on implementing an automated system that will distribute rewards in real-time, directly tied to each transaction your referrals make.

## Getting Started

Ready to join the TokenFabric Affiliation Program? Follow these simple steps:

1. Connect your wallet to the TokenFabric platform.
2. Navigate to the Affiliation Program section in your dashboard.
3. Generate your unique referral link.
4. Start sharing your link and growing your network!
