## Welcome to TokenFabric

TokenFabric is an innovative platform that democratizes the creation and management of digital assets on blockchain networks. Our protocol empowers creators, entrepreneurs, artists, and visionaries to bring their ideas to life through customizable tokenomics, without requiring coding skills or deep blockchain knowledge.

<Note>
  The TokenFabric protocol and this documentation are still in early stages and are subject to major changes.
</Note>

## Why Choose TokenFabric?

TokenFabric stands out with its user-friendly approach to blockchain innovation:

- **Accessibility**: Create and manage digital assets with no coding required
- **Flexibility**: Customize tokenomics to fit your project's unique needs
- **Security**: Smart contracts secured by ERC2535 diamond pattern for upgradeability and optimization
- **Multi-Chain Support**: Currently on Ethereum and Base, with plans to expand to all EVM-compatible networks
- **Seamless Integration**: Native bridge feature powered by Chainlink CCIP for cross-chain asset transfers
